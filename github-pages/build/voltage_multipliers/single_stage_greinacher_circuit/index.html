Conclusion
This circuit have AC as input, but output ideally is DC voltage higher than the max voltage of the input. The lower the frequency the higher the capacitance of the capacitors required to get a proper DC output. The DC output voltage is about 8.73V for ideal output when input is 5Vpp.
